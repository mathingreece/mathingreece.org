This website aims at listing, as completely as possible, announcements for the conferences, workshops and seminars in Mathematics, pure and applied, being organized within the "boundaries" of the Hellenic Mathematical Community.

The goal is twofold:

  • to create a portal where the members of the Hellenic and International Mathematical Community can be informed about the research meetings taking place in Greece and Cyprus.
  • to encourage especially undergraduate and graduate students from the eight Hellenic Departments of Mathematics to participate in such events as an initial step towards the further development of University level Mathematics in these departments.

In this direction, it would be greatly appreciated if the organizers (or participants) of any future mathematics conferences or seminars taking place in Greece or Cyprus would send the relative announcements, as well as any comments, to the e-mail address mathingreece@gmail.com in order to keep this website up to date and further develop it.
